Our Story

From a Dream to
Bangladesh's Green Leader

Eco Healing Accessories was born from a simple but powerful belief — that manufacturing and environmental responsibility don't have to be opposites. Since our founding in 2014, we have grown from a small accessories supplier into one of Bangladesh's most recognised names in sustainable garments manufacturing.

2014
Company Founded

Eco Healing Accessories established in Dhaka with a core focus on eco-friendly garments accessories for the Bangladesh export market.

2016
First International Export

Successfully delivered our first bulk order to European fashion brands, opening doors to global partnerships.

2018
GRS & GOTS Certification

Achieved Global Recycled Standard (GRS) and GOTS certification — marking a major milestone in our eco-compliance journey.

2020
Expanded Product Portfolio

Launched 50+ new eco accessory lines including recycled buttons, trims, and biodegradable packaging solutions.

2024
500+ Global Clients

Crossed 500 satisfied clients across Europe, USA, and Asia — reaffirming our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

The principles that guide every product we make and every relationship we build.

🎯

Our Mission

To manufacture world-class garments accessories using eco-friendly, recycled, and sustainable materials — delivering exceptional value to global fashion brands while minimising our environmental footprint at every step of production.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative eco-accessories brand in South Asia — pioneering a future where sustainable production is the industry standard, not the exception. We envision a circular economy where every accessory has a second life.

💎

Our Values

  • Sustainability First — Eco responsibility in every decision.
  • Quality Commitment — Zero compromise on product standards.
  • Customer Trust — Long-term partnerships over short-term gains.
  • Innovation — Constantly improving materials and processes.
  • Integrity — Transparent, honest, and ethical always.
